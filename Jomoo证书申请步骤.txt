=====================================本次使用（PKCS12）格式============================================
1、	证书库
	keytool -genkey -trustcacerts -dname "CN= yqzl.jomoo.cn,OU=Jomoo,O=Jomoo,L=Shanghai,ST=Shanghai,C=CN" -alias tomcat -keyalg RSA -keystore Jomoo_server.keystore -keypass 123456 -storepass 123456 -validity 3650
	keytool -genkey -trustcacerts -dname "CN= yqzl.jomoo.cn,OU=Jomoo,O=Jomoo,L=Shanghai,ST=Shanghai,C=CN" -alias tomcat -keyalg RSA -keystore Jomoo_server_p12.keystore -storetype PKCS12 -storepass 123456 -validity 3650 （PKCS12）
---结果文件 证书库：Jomoo_server_p12.keystore

2、	证书请求文件（加密选项：-sigalg MD5withRSA  、 -sigalg SHA1withRSA ）
	keytool -certreq -alias tomcat -file Jomoo_server.csr -keystore Jomoo_server.keystore -storepass 123456
	keytool -certreq -alias tomcat -file Jomoo_server_p12.csr -keystore Jomoo_server_p12.keystore -storetype PKCS12 -storepass 123456 （PKCS12）
----结果文件 证书请求文件：Jomoo_server_p12.csr

-- 查看
	openssl req -in Jomoo_server_p12.csr -text Cnoout
	keytool -list -v -keystore Jomoo_server_p12.keystore -storepass 123456 -storetype PKCS12
	
-- 测试
---- 签发
	openssl ca -in Jomoo_server.csr -out Jomoo_server.cer -config "/home/mbs/test/cert1/conf/testca.conf"

	cp Jomoo_server.keystore Jomoo_server_all.keystore
	cp Jomoo_server_p12.keystore Jomoo_server_all_p12.keystore

	keytool -import -trustcacerts -alias ca -file /home/mbs/test/cert1/cacert1.pem -keystore Jomoo_server_all_p12.keystore -storetype PKCS12  -storepass 123456 
	keytool -import -alias tomcat -file Jomoo_server_p12.cer -keystore Jomoo_server_all_p12.keystore -storetype PKCS12  -storepass 123456

5、	制作根证书文件（直接用根证书 Jomoo_0_WoSign.pem ）
查看：
	keytool -printcert -file 0_WoSign.cer
制作一个空的keystore（p12）：
	cp Jomoo_server_p12.keystore Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12
	keytool -list -v -keystore Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12 -storepass 123456 -storetype PKCS12
	keytool -delete -alias tomcat -keystore Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12 -storepass 123456 -storetype PKCS12
	keytool -list -v -keystore Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12 -storepass 123456 -storetype PKCS12
	keytool -import -trustcacerts -alias root -file 0_WoSign.cer -keystore Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12 -storetype PKCS12 -storepass 123456
格式转换：
	openssl pkcs12 -in Jomoo_0_WoSign.p12 -nodes -out Jomoo_0_WoSign_all.pem
	openssl x509 -in Jomoo_0_WoSign_all.pem -out Jomoo_0_WoSign.pem
